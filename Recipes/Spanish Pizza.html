Spanish Pizza

Description

A very rich and fluffy pizza to make at home

Ingredients

  • 250 g of strong flour
  • 15 g of fresh yeast
  • 150 ml of water
  • 10 g of saltLa Española extra virgin olive oil
  • 50 g of chorizo ​slices
  • 50 g of parmesan cheese
  • 1 teaspoon of basil
  • Olive oil with basil
  • Baking paper

Steps

  1. Place the sifted flour in a large bowl, forming a volcano shape. In another bowl, dissolve the yeast in water and pour it into the centre of the volcano. Mix the flour with the dissolved yeast, kneading with your hands until the ingredients are well combined. Add the salt when the dough is consistent and continue kneading.
  2. Flour a counter and knead the pizza dough with a rolling pin. When the dough no longer sticks to your fingers, form it into a ball and let it rest in the bowl covered with a damp cloth for an hour to double its volume.
  3. After resting, knead the dough again on the floured work surface. Flatten it with a rolling pin and give it the classic pizza shape, more or less thin according to your preference.
  4. Place the dough on a baking tray lined with baking paper. Add a base of fried tomato, followed by chopped ham, mozzarella and grated parmesan. Drizzle with virgin olive oil, add salt, pepper, basil, oregano, and distribute the chorizo ​​slices.
  5. Preheat the oven to 180 degrees. Bake the pizza for 15-20 minutes, depending on how golden you like your cheese.
